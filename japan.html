My Dream Trip to Japan

Must-See Tourist Spots

Japan is a country of contrasts, from ancient temples to futuristic cities.

Tokyo: The Vibrant Capital

I want to visit the famous Shibuya Crossing, the Skytree tower, and the peaceful Meiji Shrine.

Kyoto: The Heart of Tradition

Exploring the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and the Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji) is a priority.

Gastronomy: Foods I Must Try!

Japanese food is famous all over the world. This is my list!

  • Ramen: More than just a noodle soup, it's an art. I want to try tonkotsu (pork broth).
  • Sushi and Sashimi: Trying fresh fish directly from the Tsukiji Market in Tokyo.
  • Takoyaki: Batter balls filled with octopus, a street food delight from Osaka.
  • Okonomiyaki: Sometimes called "Japanese pizza," it's a type of savory grilled pancake.
